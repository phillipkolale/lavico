About Us

The  counties in focus have since experienced exponential population increase in the last decade, based on the 2019 census report. The estimate stands at 5.3 million people with high poverty level reflected by more than 80% of the total population living on income of less than one dollar per day and thus exposing the population to life threatening social economic activities. Further to this, the region bares the largest share of HIV/AIDs diseases burden with the four counties of Homabay, Siaya, Kisumu and Migori being the top four in HIV/AIDS prevalence ranking and percentage figures three times higher than national prevalence of 4.9% except for Busia with slightly elevated prevalence 5.1% compared to national average.

In addition to inherent socio- economic challenges, the county’s population remain deeply rooted in traditional cultural practices, added to strong ties with political systems whether beneficial to development agenda or not.

These circumstantial scenerios have resulted to the counties lagging behind in development agenda, with economic activities related to agriculture, environmental conservation, technology application, infrastructure and education sectors below the par compared to other regions considered as developed.

Because of these issues, Lavico-edp has come up with solutions and projects that will enable communities in the counties in focus to empower themselves all round.


Vision

Lavico – edp envisages for a healthy and wealthy empowered Population through participating in development, growth and economic sustainable programs


Mission

Lavico-edp endeavor is to promote sustainable socio economically empowered society through creative, innovative and sustainable practices of agriculture, environmental conservations, health interventions, educational growth programs, promotion of good governance and sustainable Economic inclusive development

 
Objectives

Lavico-edp's  overall objective is to promote and expand access to quality education, healthcare services, good governance, conservation of environments, Improve agricultural production, water and hygiene to the poor and vulnerable society. The organisation Specific objectives to be​accomplished within its powers and coverage include:

• To promote access to affordable quality education, instituting bursary fund support to the needy students in secondary and vocational institutions and also promote the CBC Compliance in the public Institutions within the vulnerable areas through expanding and equipping their learning structures especially in the Early Year Education (EYE) framework.

• To influence Health agenda, increase accountable resources for universal access to equal Reproductive, Maternal, New-born, Child and Adolescent Health and Nutrition (RMNCAHN) by the vulnerable groups through rights based approach and integration within the existing government interventions
including response to emerging trends/issues such COVID-19 and HIV/AIDS.

• To promote agribusiness management practices, adapt and build resilience of sustainable agricultural practices and food security systems to climatic
change at multiple levels to the vulnerable society.

• To promote proper conservation of environmental practices along Lake Victoria through training on green technology, control of pollution and human
activities among the vulnerable society.
• To provide access to safe water and proper hygienic practices among the poor society

• To advocate and promote good governance for both private and public entities for sound economic management, proper utilization of public resources, policy development through training to improve economic and livelihood of the poor society.

• To raise, secure and utilise funds for the attainment of any or all objects of the Organization, and to do such other things as are incidental or conducive to the attainment of these objects

Strategic Program Approach 

Lavico-edp focuses to meet its fiscal budget through diverse sources which include locally and internationally grants and donations. The programme targets both adults and children living with HIV/AIDS, Youths, other vulnerable categories in the population particularly the neglected elderly people, orphans and their caregivers. 
Lavico-edp targets the community in need for provisions of basic services, equipment and basic material goods combined with capacity building for enhanced life skills through training, advocacy and information advise for governance including substantiable livelihood for individual households. 
The activities and service interventions implemented by Lavico-edp are community sensitive through incorporation of development approaches that considers inclusivity, participation of all segments of the population and technological solutions for efficiency and sustainability.
